Rising Waters

An Intelligent Machine Learning-Powered Flood Susceptibility & Prediction System

Project Overview

Floods are among the most devastating natural disasters, claiming thousands of lives and displacing millions annually. Conventional forecasting methods often fall short in predicting floods at the right time, leaving authorities with insufficient opportunity to respond. Rising Waters addresses this gap using classification algorithms—specifically XGBoost (achieving 96.55% accuracy)—to predict the likelihood of flood events based on meteorological features, topography, and infrastructure scores.

Core Use Cases & Scenarios

Early Flood Warning

Enables meteorologists to enter current rainfall and cloud visibility readings to predict flooding and issue evacuation advisories hours in advance.

Disaster Response

Assists disaster relief coordinators during monsoon seasons to monitor multiple regions simultaneously and prioritize resources to high-risk zones.

Model Validation

Allows government analysts to benchmark model accuracy, view comparison metrics across algorithms, and analyze performance reports.
